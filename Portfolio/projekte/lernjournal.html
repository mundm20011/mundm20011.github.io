Lernjournal

Moderne Web-Applikation für das obligatorische Lernjournal. Ermöglicht Auszubildenden, ihre Fortschritte in Form von Artikeln zu dokumentieren.

Goal

The goal of the project, is to develop and provide a modern Web-Application for the Lerndokumentation. It should allow all apprentices to document their work, progress, experiences and guides. This is mandatory in accordance with Art. 12 Abs. 1 Bildungsverordnung (BiVo). Furthermore it is recommended to let apprentices fill out a daily journal in which they document and reflect on the work they've done on a daily basis. For the project this means that it has to support two kinds of entries. Articles and Journals whereas Articles can be made public, so that other apprentices can read and learn.

Procedure

As a first step, a MVP (Minimum Viable Product) should be developed and deployed, so that the product is used from early on, which can then be refined and improved in increments using an agile approach.
